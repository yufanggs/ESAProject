Requirements Allocations
Game Duration

Logic designer shall ensure that the game lasts < 1 hour
and the set up time will be less than 2 minutes in 95% confident level.
Durability

Artifact designer shall ensure the product (game board, pieces, cards) shall last for more than 1 year
and the product shall be waterproof.
Storability Package designer shall ensure that the package is not be larger than 15*19*2 inches dimension.
Safety Artifact designer shall ensures that the product must not contain sharp edges.
Aesthetic

Graphic artiest shall ensures that the design contains at least one cartoon characters and make use of at least 3 warm colors.

Budget Friendly Logistic person shall make sure that all necessary expenses must not exceed SGD 100.
Packaging designer shall ensure that packaging costs must not exceed SGD 20.
Graphic designer shall ensure that material costs must not exceed SGD 50.
Artifact designer shall ensure that manufacturing cost must not exceed SGD 30.
Timeline Logistic person shall make sure purchasing materials will arrive 2 days before fabrication.
Graphic design shall ensures that video filming production will be completed before week 14.
