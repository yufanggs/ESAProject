Child who reaches a landmark draws a question card and answer the designated question within limited time.
Initial condition
1. Question card is on the table.
2. Timer is off.
3. Location is a MRT station with landmarks.  
Operator (Child) Card Timer
The child draws a question card    
  The card shows question to the child.  
The child answers the question on the card.    
    Timer starts.
The child comes up with the solution or fails to answer.    
    Timer off.
  The card shows standard solution to the other player(s).  
The child puts away the card.    
Ending conditions
1. Question card is removed.
2. Timer is off.
