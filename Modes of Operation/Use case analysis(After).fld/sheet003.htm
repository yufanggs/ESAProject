Child heads back when reaching the end of the MRT line according to number shown on dice.
Initial condition  
1. Dice on the table.
2. The child's chess reaches the end of the MRT line.
Operator (Child) Dice Chess
The child rolls a dice.    
  The dice shows the number of steps to move forward.  
The child controls the chess.    
    The chess reaches the end of the MRT line with remaining steps.
The child turns the chess around.    
    The chess heads back along the same line and finishes the remaining steps.
Ending conditions  
1. Dice on the table.
2. Chess is at final destination.
