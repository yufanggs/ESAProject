In the beginning of a game, children rolls a colored dice to determine their starting points along the corresponding MRT lines.
Initial condition
1. Game ready to start.
2. Chesses are with players.
3. The colored dice is on the table.  
Operator (Child) Dice Chess
The child rolls the colored dice.    
  The dice shows the color associated with a MRT line.  
The child puts the chess on the starting point on that MRT line.    
    Chesses are landed on starting points on the board.
Ending conditions  
1. Game starts.
1. Chesses are landed on starting points on the board.
2. The colored dice is on the table.
