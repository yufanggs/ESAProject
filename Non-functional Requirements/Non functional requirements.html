Non-functional Requirements

Requirements Description
Conceptual Integrity

The overall design shall be consistent and coherent, suitable for children with
the aim to educate them about historical geography knowledge.
Durability

The product shall not break when the board is dropped from a height of at most 1 meter.
The product shall be waterproof.
Storability The product shall be able to fit into a box of 15*19*2 inches dimension.
Safety The products shall not include sharp edges.
Aesthetic

The product shall include cartoons characters.
The board shall consist of at least 3 colours from the warm colour scheme.

Budget Friendly The cost of the product shall not exceed $50.
