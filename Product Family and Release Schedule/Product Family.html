Product Family and Release Schedule

The team has decided to be risk-averse and implement the platform strategy in two stages, moving horizontally first and then going up by digitalization.

Stage 1 would be based on the same general topic historical geography but achieve the learning results by integrating different routes. It will be launched within 3 years of the initial standard product release.

From here, judging from the product performance, we would choose between moving horizontally to stage 2 or digitalize the most successful product in stage 1, which shall be achieved from 3 to 5 years onwards.
